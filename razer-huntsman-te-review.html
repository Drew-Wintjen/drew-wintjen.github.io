Razer Huntsman TE

Quick take:

A wonderful keyboard to type with. The keys have nice clack sound and are very quick. Plus PBT keycaps included!!!

The Huntsman TE by Razer

The Razer Huntsman series has been Razer’s flagship, high-end keyboard line-up. Recently they came out with a tournament edition of the keyboard, which what they did was just remove the tenkey. Before this keyboard, I honestly never thought of owning any razer products that wasn’t a mousepad because of the stigma they had from their past with quality control issues. One thing I can say for sure is that this keyboard is one of the highest quality big brand keyboard.

Cons

This keyboard is very good. So, finding cons are very hard. One thing that is lacking is the RGB. Which is very funny because it’s a Razer product. Normally they are lit up more than a Christmas tree. It has all the RGB modes they have to offer just with some of the keys like the caps key, shift, enter, and backspace. The RGB doesn’t illuminate the whole key. Which isn’t a big deal to me, but I know that could bother some people. Another con of this keyboard is also a pro. When I got this keyboard, I wasn’t expecting it to be so sensitive on the keys.

So, for the first couple months I was having several typing errors because I would accidently hit keys I didn’t want. But as time went on, I got use to it and really like it.

Pros

I do really like this keyboard. It’s probably my favorite keyboard I’ve owned. Let’s start with what’s made of. The body is primarily plastic, and it has good strength. I don’t notice any board flex as I type. One think that was a huge pro is that this is one of the only keyboards that are made by a major peripheral company that ships with PBT keycaps.

PBT is much better than ABS for one simple reason. It doesn’t get the sheen on the keycaps. Resulting in better grip and feel for a lot longer. Another pro is the switches they use. It’s razer’s custom laser switches. Which are superfast and responsive.

Conclusion

Overall, I personally love this keyboard. Keyboards are all about personal preference. I think its fun to type on this keyboard. Its sound and feel are like nothing I have ever used. I think I might have found my forever keyboard with the Razer Huntsman TE.
