Sennheiser PC-37x

Quick take:

With the iconic sound of Sennheiser. These gaming headphones are no joke. They are one of my favorites and that's for good reason.

The PC-37x by Sennheiser

Sennheiser is one of the most well know audio companies in the world. The German based company is known for its amazing audiophile headphones. So, it makes sense that when they step their feet into the gaming market. They have great success. The Massdrop Sennheiser PC-37x is my favorite of the gaming line Sennheiser has to offer. They feature one of the best microphones stock on a gaming headset I have ever had. They are very light but pack a punch in the audio department.

Cons

This headset is very. It’s honestly hard to find a con for this guy. The only one I can think of is the material the earcups are made of. It a felt material that holds hair and dust very well. Making cleaning them and must if you want to keep a nice clean looking going. Luckily these are removable but can be a hassle to put back on. When you are trying to click them in. There is no click, so it may feel like they’re in but then they fall out.

Pros

To start the audio quality is very good. No surprise. It is a good balance between the lows and highs. I wouldn’t really listen to music with them, but for gaming they are extremely good. Directional sound is very good in these.

Another thing I like was the weight. With most the headset being plastic with a metal band in the middle. It makes it feel like nothing on the head after wearing it for hours.

Along with that band it has a great clamping pressure on your head. So, with the weight down and the fact that they are an open back headset. Means it lets your ears breath and cool. The last thing that I want to make a point on is the microphone.

It has a rich deep sound, that compared to other gaming headsets in this price range. Is very uncompressed and sounds a lot more nature than lets say Hyperx headsets or Logitech. My favorite feature of the microphone is the mute function. To mute the mic all you have to do is tilt the mic up, until it clicks, and your muted.

Conclusion

This Sennheiser headset is the real deal. Coming in at $100 on Drop.com. You will not be disappointed. This destroys every headset at $100 or less. No question about. Highly recommend.
